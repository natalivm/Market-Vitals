The read

AVGO sits at 370.83, mid-range: below the 50-day (391.60) and the daily Bollinger mid (383.82), above the 200-day (350.72) support shelf. That is a balance / no-man’s-land — no edge from here. The two spots that pay: a reaction at the 350–356 support (reclaim + impulse = long), or a reclaim of 391 → 409 that flips the near-term structure up. Losing 350 opens 323.

Watch — re-accumulation at 350–356: a tight balance holding on the support shelf (200-day 350.72 · BB-lower 355.91 · the $360 put wall) would be constructive re-accumulation before a next leg up — time at support = unfilled buy orders stacking. Confirm it, don’t predict it: it must hold 350 (a decisive close below flips it to distribution → 323), show absorption (long lower wicks, volume, little downside progress), then trigger on an impulse that reclaims 374 → 380–383. In AVGO’s negative-gamma regime, that break can run.
