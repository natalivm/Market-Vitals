ℹ️ How to read this — flow leads, price lagstap to expand

Price ≠ flow. Price is set by whoever is most urgent right now; flow is the net dollars institutions positioned over the last 5 sessions. They can point opposite ways — and that gap is the signal.

Money in while price falls = 💰 buying the dip. Institutions absorb panic selling cheaply. Their bids support but don't lift the price yet — the move comes later, once sellers exhaust. Flow leads, price follows.

Money out while price rises = ⚠️ selling into strength. Smart money unloads into momentum buyers. The tape looks fine, but the support has already left — so a sector "holding up" is a warning, not comfort.

It's a thesis, not a law: if the flow flips (e.g. semis' inflow turns negative), the divergence has resolved — that's the moment to watch for.
